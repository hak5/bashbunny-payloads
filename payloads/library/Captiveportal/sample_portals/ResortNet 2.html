WORLDMARK GUEST ACCESS TO Wi-Fi: FAQ

Top 4 things a user should do to connect to Wi-Fi
  1. Make sure the WiFi switch on your laptop or other device is set to "On".
  2. Make sure that you are connecting to the correct network name in the WiFi settings of your device = ResortNet2.
  3. Once connected, open a web browser to complete the process and you will be redirected to ResortNet2 Login landing page. If you are not automatically redirected to a login page, please try going to a different website and you should then be sent to the correct page.
  4. If you successfully connect to WiFi and get disconnected, please retry connecting using the Existing User login process since your connection may have been automatically terminated due to inactivity of the device.

Top 5 things a user should do to register/sign in and get online with ResortNet 2:
  1. The ResortNet2 usernames are different than the legacy ResortNet usernames. If you have not used ResortNet2 to access the Internet in the past, you will need to create a new account including username and password.
  2. Your username is usually your email address unless you have changed it, and your password must be (6) characters long.
  3. If you are a Travelshare Member, access is complimentary, but you will need your Owner ID, Billing last name, State and Zip from your Member account to validate enrollment.
    • If you need help or details on your account, please call Owner Care at (888) 648-7363
  4. Club Wyndham guests staying in Club Wyndham units need to enter the voucher code issued to them during check-in when signing up for their wireless plan. Club Wyndham guests staying on Wyndham Club Pass do not enter a voucher number. Club Pass guests will follow the same WiFi guidelines as non-Travelshare WorldMark owners.
  5. All non-Travelshare Worldmark owners and guests must sign up for paid usage, so please be prepared to enter credit card information in order to gain access to WiFi.
